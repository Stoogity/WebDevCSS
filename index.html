Welcome to the home boulder wall! Power training is important - you should find a climb that has an early crux (difficult movement) that you can't quite execute. I've spent time on this problem and so I'm able to execute fairly consistently now. At the start this was not the case. Always aim to work harder movements in each training cycle.
